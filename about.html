About us

Macunique Trainers Limited is a Kaduna based training firm consisting of Board of Advisors and Associate Consultants. The Firm is duly registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission Abuja with RC 989011. It has a committed and highly experienced team comprising Eight Board of Advisors, 16 Associate Consultants and Faculty Members in various disciplines within and outside the country, ranging from Agriculture, Entrepreneurial and Commercialization Development, Organizational Development, Human Resources, Project Management, and Monitoring and Evaluation. The firm has undertaken several projects and training successfully, among such is the management and good governance training programmes, International Tertiary Institution Top Executive and Stakeholder (ITITES)programmes, legislative Programmes,Entrepreneurial and Commercialization programmes in various countries such as; Malaysia, China, Dubai USA and some Africa countries. Macunique Trainers Limited has a multi-disciplinary base of consultants that allows several perspectives to be given consideration in their programmes.
